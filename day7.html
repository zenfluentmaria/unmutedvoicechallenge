The minimum

The standard

Three reps per day is where real installation begins. Your nervous system starts to recognize the sequence as familiar. The gap score starts to move. The default starts to shift.

Before you speak

The IBS Activation Sequence

🪪
Identity: Who am I in this room — honestly?
🎯
Being / Role: Am I the advisor, seller, decision maker, update giver?
🌬️
State: Name it. Notice it. Breathe it out. Feet on the floor.
📍
Structure: What is my one main point — decided before I open my mouth?
▶
When you speak

The CEO Framework

C
Core message first. One sentence. Lead with it. Do not build to it.
E
Explain with one thing. One reason, example, or way. Then stop.
O
Own the ending. Return to the point, state the conclusion, or ask the direct question. Do not trail off.
✓

The Shape of This Challenge

Two Phases.
One Transformation.

This challenge is not 30 identical days. It has a shape — and knowing the shape tells you exactly what your job is from here.

1
Phase 1 · Days 1–7 · Awareness & Map
You are here. This week was about building the right map — understanding your gap, naming your triggers, learning the tools. IBS. CEO. The marching drill. The gap score. Every day this week gave you a piece of the system. Today is the close of that foundation.
You leave Phase 1 with the map, the method, and an honest baseline score. That is enough to begin.
2
Phase 2 · Days 8–30 · Installation
Twenty-three days. One job: reps. No new content. No new frameworks. Just the techniques — used daily, logged daily, built into your default. This is where identity actually shifts. Not by reading about it. By doing it until the new version of you is the one who shows up automatically.
Your daily practice: Choose at least 1 activity per day — 2–3 is the standard. Run it in a real speaking moment. Score it. Log it in your tracker. That's the whole job. Repeat until Day 30.
The tracker is your evidence. Each rep you log is proof the new identity is becoming your default — not an effort, not a performance. The reps are the work. Everything else is preparation for the reps.
